AI a la Carte

The Practical Use of AI in the Workplace

Date
Tuesday, 29 September 2026
Time
13:00 – 14:00
Format
Hands-on Workshop

Join us for a hands-on session where the group decides what to build together using AI. The aim is to demonstrate how AI tools can augment work and boost productivity across functions, all grounded in the responsible, ethical, and transparent use of AI.

You pick from the menu. We build it. Together. Live.

The Menu

Five dishes. You choose which one we cook up together.

Dish 01

Create

The Lesson Launcher

The room picks a topic nobody here teaches. Together we build a complete 1-hour lesson plan with learning outcomes, activities, and assessment. From blank page to finished resource.

~15 min
Dish 02

Communicate

The Tone Shifter

Take one real message and rewrite it for three different audiences: student, parent, senior management. Same content, three registers. Audience adaptation in action.

~15 min
Dish 03

Analyse

The Survey Whisperer

Feed in anonymised free-text feedback (student evaluations, staff surveys) and extract themes, quantify sentiment, and surface the three things to act on. Raw text to actionable insight.

~15 min
Dish 04

Automate

The Monday Morning Machine

A volunteer maps out a real recurring weekly task. We build a reusable prompt template that turns 45 minutes of drudgery into 5. Walk away with something you can use next Monday.

~15 min
Dish 05

Learn

The Instant Expert

Someone names a topic outside their expertise. We build a structured self-study guide with key concepts, common misconceptions, and questions to test understanding. AI as tutor, not crutch.

~15 min
